# Renovation Pitfall Avoidance Skills

**Renovation Pitfall Avoidance Skills** is a curated set of Chinese home renovation knowledge skills for AI coding assistants and Agent frameworks. Based on 20 years of practical experience from the "从学到装｜装修课堂" knowledge base, it covers 11 high-frequency pitfall avoidance scenarios across the entire renovation lifecycle.

## Tags

🏠 Home & Renovation | ✅ Verified

## Core Philosophy

The skill set emphasizes:
- **Knowledge-base-first**: 80% of conclusions are derived from the real renovation knowledge base, reducing model hallucination.
- **Homeowner perspective**: Protects homeowner interests throughout budgeting, contracts, material selection, construction, and acceptance.
- **Actionable**: Each skill provides executable checklists, question templates, and judgment criteria.

## Key Features & Workflow

1. **Contract Review**: Identify deposit traps, payment ratios, liability clauses, and verbal promise risks.
2. **Quote Review**: Spot missing items, double charging, inflated unit prices, and vague material descriptions.
3. **Plan Diagnosis**: Evaluate renovation plans based on layout, budget, and needs.
4. **Whole-House Customization**: Avoid pitfalls in boards, hardware, pricing methods, and contracts.
5. **Old House Renovation**: Demolition, electrical/plumbing, structural changes, and budget control.
6. **Eco-Friendly Pitfall Avoidance**: Formaldehyde treatment, children's room material selection, and environmental rating judgment.
7. **Process Management**: Work sequence, schedule, and acceptance timing.
8. **Renovation Rescue**: Remedies for tile, wall, electrical, and customization failures.
9. **Quick Q&A**: Instant answers to common renovation questions.
10. **Extra-Charge Avoidance**: Identify hidden charges and price-hike tactics.
11. **Company Selection**: Avoid shell companies and identify reliable renovation contractors.
