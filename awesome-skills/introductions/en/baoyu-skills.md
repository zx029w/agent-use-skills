# Baoyu Skills

**Baoyu Skills** is a practical skill collection for AI coding agents like Claude Code, shared by Baoyu to boost daily work efficiency. It covers content creation, image generation, social media publishing, document processing, and more.

## Tags

🎨 Design & Creativity | ✅ Verified

## Core Philosophy
The design of Baoyu Skills emphasizes:
- **Content as Productivity**: Fully automates the lifecycle of producing and publishing articles, images, and social media content.
- **Multi-Platform Coverage**: Supports publishing to mainstream platforms including Xiaohongshu (RedNote), WeChat Official Accounts, Weibo, and X (Twitter).
- **Flexible & Extensible**: EXTEND.md mechanism allows project-level and user-level customization to fit team and individual workflows.
- **Multi-Backend Support**: Core features like image generation support a wide range of AI providers including OpenAI, Google, DashScope, MiniMax, and Replicate.

## Key Features & Workflow

1. **Content Creation & Visualization**:
   - `baoyu-xhs-images`: Xiaohongshu infographic series generator with 9 visual styles and 6 layouts.
   - `baoyu-infographic`: Professional infographic generator with 20 layout types and 17 visual styles.
   - `baoyu-cover-image`: Article cover image generator using a 5-dimension system (Type × Palette × Rendering × Text × Mood).
   - `baoyu-slide-deck`: Generates slide deck images from content and automatically merges them into .pptx and .pdf files.
   - `baoyu-comic`: Knowledge comic creator with flexible art style × tone combinations.
   - `baoyu-article-illustrator`: Smart article illustration skill that auto-analyzes content structure and inserts visuals.

2. **Social Media Publishing**:
   - `baoyu-post-to-x`: Posts content to X (Twitter), supporting regular posts and X Articles (long-form Markdown).
   - `baoyu-post-to-wechat`: Posts to WeChat Official Accounts in image-text or full article mode, with multi-account support.
   - `baoyu-post-to-weibo`: Posts to Weibo, supporting regular posts and headline articles.

3. **AI Image Generation**:
   - `baoyu-imagine`: Multi-backend AI image generation supporting OpenAI, Azure, Google, DashScope, MiniMax, Jimeng, Seedream, and Replicate. Supports reference images, batch generation, and custom sizes.
   - `baoyu-danger-gemini-web`: Interacts with Gemini Web to generate text and images.

4. **Content Utilities**:
   - `baoyu-youtube-transcript`: Downloads YouTube transcripts with multi-language, translation, chapters, and speaker identification support.
   - `baoyu-url-to-markdown`: Fetches any URL via Chrome CDP and converts to clean Markdown.
   - `baoyu-danger-x-to-markdown`: Converts X (Twitter) tweets and articles to Markdown format.
   - `baoyu-compress-image`: Compresses images to reduce file size while maintaining quality.
   - `baoyu-format-markdown`: Formats Markdown files with proper frontmatter, titles, summaries, headings, and more.
   - `baoyu-markdown-to-html`: Converts Markdown to styled HTML with WeChat-compatible themes.
   - `baoyu-translate`: Multi-mode translation tool with quick, normal, and refined workflows.

## Skills Library Overview
- **Content Creation**: Xiaohongshu graphics, infographics, cover images, slide decks, comics, article illustrations.
- **Social Publishing**: Automated publishing to X (Twitter), WeChat Official Accounts, and Weibo.
- **Image Generation**: Unified interface to multiple mainstream AI image generation providers.
- **Content Utilities**: YouTube transcripts, URL-to-markdown, format conversion, image compression, article translation.
