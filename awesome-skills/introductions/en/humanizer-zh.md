# Humanizer-zh (AI Writing Remover - Chinese Enhanced)

**Humanizer-zh** is a professional writing editing skill optimized specifically for the Chinese context. It is designed to identify and eliminate obvious signs of AI generation in text. Based on Wikipedia's comprehensive "Signs of AI writing" guide and deep adaptation for Chinese writing habits, it makes AI-generated text sound more natural and human-like.

## Tags

🗂️ Document & Office | ✅ Verified

## Core Philosophy

- **De-AIification**: Precisely identifies common Chinese AI "tics" (e.g., "Additionally," "In summary," "Crucial"), overused punctuation (e.g., em dashes —), and hollow promotional rhetoric.
- **Injecting Soul**: Rejects sterile, opinion-less statements. By introducing specific personal views, flexible narrative rhythms, and authentic emotional shifts, it makes the writing feel like it comes from a real person.
- **Specific Over Abstract**: Replaces AI-favored "milestone initiatives" with concrete "resolved problem X in project Y," winning back reader trust with details.

## Key Features & Workflow

1. **AI Pattern Recognition**: Scans and identifies patterns including inflated symbolism, superficial participle phrase analysis, vague attributions, and more.
2. **Natural Rewriting**: Rewrites stiff copula avoidance (substituting simple "is" with complex "marks the...") into concise and direct modern Chinese expressions.
3. **Authenticity Enhancement**: Introduces first-person perspective at appropriate times, acknowledges complexity and uncertainty, and earns reader trust with specific details instead of generalizations.
4. **Quality Assessment**: Provides a multi-dimensional scoring system to evaluate the effectiveness of humanization across five dimensions: Directness, Rhythm, Trust, Authenticity, and Refinement.

## Skills Library Overview

- **Content Patterns**: Identifies and fixes over-emphasis on significance, notability, and formulaic "Challenges and Outlook" structures.
- **Language & Grammar**: Built-in high-frequency AI vocabulary (Chinese version) and correction rules for negative parallelism, rule of three overuse, etc.
- **Style & Formatting**: Specialized optimization for em dashes, boldface, inline-header lists, and quotation mark habits.
