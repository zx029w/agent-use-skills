# Content Research Writer

**Content Research Writer** is a collaborative writing skill that acts as your writing partner, helping you research, outline, draft, and refine content while preserving your unique voice and style.

## Tags

🗂️ Document & Office | ✅ Verified

## Core Philosophy
- **Collaborate, Not Replace**: Offer suggestions and options rather than overriding your writing.
- **Preserve Your Voice**: Learn your writing style and enhance it, not change it.
- **Iterative Refinement**: Support multiple rounds of revision to progressively improve quality.
- **Evidence-Driven Writing**: Research and add reliable citations and data to support claims.

## Key Features & Workflow

1. **Collaborative Outlining**: Helps you structure ideas into coherent outlines, identify research gaps, and ensure logical flow.
2. **Research Assistance**: Finds relevant information from credible sources, extracts key facts and data, and adds proper citations.
3. **Hook Improvement**: Analyzes and strengthens your opening with multiple alternatives (data-driven, story-based, question-based, etc.).
4. **Section Feedback**: Provides real-time reviews of each section as you write, covering clarity, flow, evidence, and style.
5. **Voice Preservation**: Learns and maintains your writing style and tone, ensuring the final piece reads like your own work.
6. **Citation Management**: Supports inline citations, numbered references, and footnotes, while maintaining a running reference list.
7. **Iterative Refinement**: Helps improve writing quality through multiple drafts and comprehensive reviews, including a pre-publish checklist.

## Use Cases

- Writing blog posts, articles, or newsletters
- Creating educational content or tutorials
- Drafting thought leadership pieces
- Researching and writing case studies
- Producing technical documentation with proper sources
- Adapting content for different audiences

## Writing Workflows

The skill supports several specialized writing workflows:

- **Blog Post**: Collaborative outline → Research key points → Write sections with feedback → Final polish
- **Newsletter**: Brainstorm hooks → Quick outline → Draft in one session → Review for clarity → Quick polish
- **Technical Tutorial**: Outline steps → Write code examples → Add explanations → Test instructions → Troubleshooting section
- **Thought Leadership**: Explore unique angle → Research existing perspectives → Develop thesis → Write with strong POV → Compelling conclusion
