# Twitter Algorithm Optimizer

**Twitter Algorithm Optimizer** is a skill built on insights from Twitter's open-source algorithm. It analyzes and optimizes tweet content to maximize reach and engagement.

## Tags

🗂️ Documents & Office | 🔍 Pending Verification

## Core Philosophy

- **Algorithm Transparency**: Leverages Twitter's open-sourced mechanisms (e.g., Real-graph, SimClusters) to reveal the logic behind content distribution.
- **Engagement-Driven**: Boosts tweet ranking in the feed by optimizing interaction signals like Likes, Replies, Retweets, and Bookmarks.
- **Community Resonance**: Focuses on aligning content with specific communities (SimClusters) rather than seeking generalized, low-quality reach.

## Key Features & Workflow

1. **Tweet Draft Analysis**: Evaluates tweet performance against Twitter's ranking mechanisms and identifies potential algorithmic risks.
2. **Content Optimization & Rewriting**: Adjusts wording, structure, and call-to-action (CTA) triggers to increase ranking weight.
3. **Distribution Strategy Recommendations**: Provides strategies based on Real-graph (follower engagement) and TwHIN (topic-user fit).
4. **Negative Signal Prevention**: Identifies and avoids content likely to cause downranking, muting, or reporting.

## Skills Library Overview

- **Core Ranking Models**: Covers Real-graph (interaction prediction), SimClusters (community detection), TwHIN (topic mapping), and Tweepcred (reputation scoring).
- **Engagement Signal Mapping**: Detailed analysis of explicit (Like, Reply, RT) and implicit (Click, Dwell time) signal weights.
- **Industry Optimization Templates**: Provides optimized solutions for developers, product launches, opinion pieces, and more.
