# Colleague Skill

**Colleague Skill** is a tool that distills a colleague into an AI Skill by automatically collecting Feishu/DingTalk data to generate a digital clone with both work capabilities and personality traits.

## Core Philosophy
- **Comprehensive Recreation**: Restores not only work skills but also the unique personality, expression logic, and decision-making patterns of the colleague.
- **Dynamic Evolution**: Supports appending new files, conversational corrections, and version management to continuously enrich the digital persona.
- **Multi-Source Integration**: Seamlessly combines enterprise chat histories, internal documents, and manual characteristics input.

## Key Features & Workflow
1. **Multi-Modal Data Collection**: Automates fetching from Feishu and DingTalk, and parses chat screenshots or emails.
2. **Dual-Layer Architecture (Work & Persona)**: Separates technical assets (workflow, expertise) from emotional connections (catchphrases, attitudes) for layered generation.
3. **Conversational Evolution**: Enables direct semantic corrections (e.g., "He wouldn't say that") for local updates without breaking existing capabilities.

## Skills Library Overview
- **Creation & Management**: Use `/create-colleague` to build your colleague.
- **Update & Deletion**: Use commands like `/update-colleague` and `/list-colleagues` to maintain your digital colleague repository.
